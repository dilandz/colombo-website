Experience the Art of the West Coast

Nestled on the western coast of the island, Colombo is a vibrant metropolis where rich colonial heritage seamlessly blends with modern development. From bustling street markets to serene oceanfront promenades, the city offers a sensory overload of sights, sounds, and flavors.

Whether you are looking to dive into centuries of history, indulge in world-class shopping, or taste authentic Sri Lankan cuisine, this guide will help you uncover the very best that Colombo has to offer.

Must-Visit Neighborhoods

  • Fort: The historic center featuring the Dutch Hospital and colonial architecture.
  • Pettah: The famous open-air market for spices, textiles, and electronics.
  • Cinnamon Gardens (Colombo 07): The upscale residential area with wide, leafy streets.
  • lave Island: A trendy hub for cafes and the Beira Lake.

Top 5 Things to Do

  1. Watch the sunset at Galle Face Green while eating Isso Wade.
  2. Take a boat ride on the Beira Lake near Seema Malaka temple.
  3. Visit the National Museum of Colombo to see ancient royal regalia.
  4. Explore the Jami Ul-Alfar Mosque (The Red Mosque) in Pettah.
  5. Enjoy a high tea at the historic Galle Face Hotel.
